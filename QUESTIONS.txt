### **Question 1: Fundamentals of ADCs (SNDR, SFDR, and Quantization Noise)**

**(b)** Assuming the quantization noise is white, what is the Power Spectral Density (PSD) of the quantization noise? (Express your answer in terms of $\text{V}^2/\text{Hz}$).
**(c)** You test a physical prototype of this ADC in the lab using a full-scale sine wave. The output spectrum analyzer reads the fundamental signal power as $0\text{ dBFS}$ (dB relative to full scale). You observe a 3rd harmonic spur at $-65\text{ dBFS}$, a 5th harmonic spur at $-72\text{ dBFS}$, and the integrated electronic noise floor (excluding the harmonics) measures $-62\text{ dBFS}$. 
Calculate the **SFDR**, the **SNDR (SINAD)**, and the true **ENOB** of this physical ADC. *(Hint: Convert dBFS values to linear power ratios to sum the distortion and noise, then convert back to dB).*

### **Question 3: Cap-DAC + SAR ADC (Bottom-Plate Sampling)**
